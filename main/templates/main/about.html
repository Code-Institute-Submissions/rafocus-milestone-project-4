Help

Create an account:

To create and account you go register, then create a new account using a username, email, and password.

Create an ticket:

You can create two types of tickets, features or bugs. Go to New Ticket, fill in the form with informations then submit.

Features:

When you request a new feature, the admin will give a quote for it to be implemented, users can vote and comment on featues to show their interest, they can donate amounts of a minimum of 5 euros, when the target funding is reached then the implementations will start.

Bugs:

The submission of bugs is free of chage, users can vote and comment on the bugs tickets to show that they are affected by it and contribute with more details.

Payments:

You can pay using a debit or credit card, all major cards are supported.
